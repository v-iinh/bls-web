Questions



Who Are We ?

Web Development Club at Boston Latin is committed to fostering a collaborative and educational environment where students can explore and learn front-end design. Through a series of resources that we provide, we hope to encourage members to build their own portfolios, games, and websites.

Do I Need To Be Good At Coding ?

Inclusivity is a core value in BLS Web, we welcome all programmers to join us. All meetings will be summarized and put onto the website, so even if you miss a meeting, you can still catch up! We hope to create a safe environment to learn and improve, so even if you're experienced, there's always room to be better.

When Do You Guys Meet ?

Meetings will be held in room 306 every Monday in Dr.Nguyen's room. Future meetings or cancelled ones will always be reported via email. With any questions, get in touch with any of the board members.

How Can I Get Started ?

Getting started with us is very easy, we prioritize helping members make their first very own projects! Navigating to the resources page will lead you to various materials, including events we participate in, and guides on how to begin coding.
