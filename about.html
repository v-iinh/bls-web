Mission Statement



The Boston Latin School Web Development Club is a space for BLS scholars to explore and learn about front-end web development. A revival of the original web development club, which ceased operations after senior members graduated, our hope is to provide the resources necessary for scholars to build their own portfolios, games, websites, and ultimately, to act as a community that collaborates to educate students about web development. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced programmer, all are welcome!

If there are any questions, you can contact us through our email, bls.webdevclub@gmail.com, or alternatively, you can ask one of our hard-working officers.

Who Are We ?

Web Development Club at Boston Latin is committed to fostering a collaborative and educational environment where students can explore and learn front-end design. Through a series of resources that we provide, we hope to encourage members to build their own portfolios, games, and websites.

Do I Need To Be Good At Coding ?

Inclusivity is a core value in BLS Web, we welcome all programmers to join us. All meetings will be summarized and put onto the website, so even if you miss a meeting, you can still catch up! We hope to create a safe environment to learn and improve, so even if you're experienced, there's always room to be better.

When Do You Guys Meet ?

Meetings will be held in room 306 every Monday in Dr.Nguyen's room. Future meetings or cancelled ones will always be reported via email. With any questions, get in touch with any of the board members.

How Can I Get Started ?

We prioritize helping members make their first very own projects! Navigating to the resources page will lead you to various materials, including events we participate in, and guides on how to begin coding.
